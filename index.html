Our Story

A Church Built on The Word

The Lord's Shepherd Ministries is an Apostolic and a prophetic Bible-believing community committed to transforming lives through the love of Christ. We exist to worship God, grow in His Word, and serve our community and the world.

✝

Faith

We are a purpose driven church, grooming the Youths to find their purpose and growing in it.

🕊

Community

We are a family — welcoming all who seek God, regardless of background or season of life.

🌿

Purpose

Every believer is called and equipped to live a life of meaning, impact, and kingdom significance.
